BERSERKERs are brutal fighters that go into combat with very little regard for their own safety. Build your Rage counter by landing successful standard attacks. Once the Rage counter is above 5, BERSERKER gains access to a variety of buffs. Use the Rage you've built to access Special Abilities through the Rage command:
SHOULDER CHARGE: Attack with moderate attack damage
BLOODLUST: Player gains HP 2.5x the damage done with the attack
CHAOS BURST: Attack with high attack damage
